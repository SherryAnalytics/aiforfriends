About Us

Friends, not
experts.

AI for Friends is a community built for people who are curious about artificial intelligence but don't know where to start — and that's perfectly okay. We believe the best way to learn is alongside people you trust.

No jargon. No gatekeeping. No pressure. Just real conversations, useful tools, and a friendly group helping each other figure out how to make AI work in everyday life.

Whether you're completely new or have just started poking around, you belong here.
